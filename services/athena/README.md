# Athena — Wiki Service

**Goddess of Wisdom**

In Greek mythology, Athena (Ἀθηνᾶ) sprang fully grown from the head of Zeus — goddess of wisdom, strategic warfare, and crafts. She is the patron of knowledge, learning, and civilization.

**Why Athena?**

This microservice is a markdown wiki engine — storing, organizing, and retrieving knowledge. Like Athena who embodies wisdom and strategic knowledge, this service is the knowledge base for the Complete Automate ecosystem.

**Domain:** Knowledge management, search, file attachments
**Dependencies:** Clio (DynamoDB), Atlas (S3)
