# Iris — Email Service

**Goddess of Messages and Rainbows**

Iris (Ἶρις) was the divine messenger of the gods and personification of the rainbow. She travelled between the heavens and earth at lightning speed, delivering messages and commands from the gods.

**Why Iris?**

This microservice sends emails — from transactional notifications to contact form responses. Like Iris who delivered messages swiftly across the divide between realms, this service carries messages from the system to its users.

**Domain:** Email delivery (SMTP)
**Dependencies:** Clio (DynamoDB), Zoho SMTP
