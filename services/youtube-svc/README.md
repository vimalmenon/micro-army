# Orpheus — YouTube Service

**Musician who could charm all living things**

Orpheus (Ὀρφεύς) was the legendary musician, poet, and prophet whose music could charm wild beasts, move trees and rocks, and even soften the hearts of Hades and Persephone. He ventured into the underworld armed only with his lyre.

**Why Orpheus?**

This microservice manages YouTube video metadata, scheduling, and uploads. Like Orpheus whose music could move mountains, this service creates and publishes video content that moves audiences.

**Domain:** Video metadata, scheduling, YouTube API integration
**Dependencies:** Clio (DynamoDB), Atlas (S3)
